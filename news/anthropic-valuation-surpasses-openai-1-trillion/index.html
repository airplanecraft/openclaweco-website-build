Anthropic's Secondary Market Valuation Soars to $1 Trillion, Surpassing OpenAI

April 23, 2026 · News
anthropicopenaigenerative aiai valuationsecondary market

AI startup Anthropic's secondary market valuation has reportedly surged to approximately $1 trillion on private equity trading platforms such as Forge Global. This valuation significantly surpasses that of OpenAI, which stands at $880 billion.

The rapid increase in Anthropic's valuation is primarily attributed to intense competition among buyers for its shares in the secondary market. As the availability of Anthropic's stock on these platforms becomes scarcer, investors are actively vying to acquire them, driving up the company's overall market capitalization. This development underscores strong investor confidence in Anthropic's future prospects and highlights the escalating competitive landscape among leading players in the artificial intelligence sector.

OpenMythos

OpenMythos is an open-source, theoretical implementation of the Anthropic Claude Mythos model, constructed by kyegomez based on publicly available research and speculation. It employs a Recurrent-Depth Transformer (RDT) architecture with three stages: a Prelude, a looped Recurrent Block, and a Coda. The core Recurrent Block allows for multiple iterations to achieve deep reasoning, enabling compute-adaptive and depth-variable reasoning. It supports switchable attention mechanisms (MLA/GQA) and utilizes a sparse Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) with routed and shared experts. OpenMythos aims to deliver performance comparable to models with higher parameter counts through greater parameter efficiency.
