OpenAI Unveils Upgraded Codex, Envisioning a Desktop 'Super AI Application' with Full App Control

April 17, 2026 · News
codexchatgptai agentdesktop aiopenai

OpenAI has recently announced an upgraded version of its AI programming tool, Codex. According to official statements, the new version of Codex now possesses a significant capability: it can seamlessly access and operate all applications (apps) installed on a user's computer. This development represents a crucial step forward for Codex in enabling broader automation and cross-application interaction, greatly expanding its potential utility in real-world user scenarios.

Concurrently, OpenAI is accelerating an ambitious initiative to develop a desktop-based "super AI application." This prospective application aims to deeply integrate three of the company's core AI technology products: the renowned conversational model ChatGPT, the newly upgraded Codex, and the Atlas browser. This strategic move signals OpenAI's commitment to elevating AI capabilities from isolated tools to a unified, intelligent platform capable of comprehensive control over the desktop environment, potentially transforming how users interact with their computers and laying a more robust foundation for advanced AI agents.
