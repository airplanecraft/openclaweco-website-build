South Korea Deploys Thousands of ChatGPT-Enabled Social Care Robots to Aid Aging Population

April 7, 2026 · News
chatgptai robotssocial careelderly care

South Korea is embarking on a significant initiative to address its rapidly aging population by deploying thousands of ChatGPT-enabled social care robots. This strategic move aims to leverage advanced artificial intelligence to support the country's elderly citizens.

The deployment is particularly critical as individuals aged 65 and over now constitute approximately 20% of South Korea's total population of 51 million. These AI-powered robots, integrated with generative AI capabilities like ChatGPT, are designed to offer a range of services including companionship, interactive communication, reminders, and potentially emergency assistance, thereby alleviating the strain on human caregivers and enhancing the quality of life for seniors.

This program highlights South Korea's proactive approach to utilizing AI agent technology to mitigate demographic challenges, setting a precedent for other nations facing similar societal shifts.
