Melania Trump Unveils AI Humanoid Robot Figure 03 at White House Global Education Summit

March 26, 2026 · News
aihumanoid robotfigure aieducation technologywhite house

Washington D.C., March 26, 2026 – Former U.S. First Lady Melania Trump captured global attention at the "Fostering the Future Together" global initiative summit at the White House by making a public appearance alongside Figure 03, an advanced AI humanoid robot. The event focused on empowering children through education, innovation, and technology, with a significant emphasis on artificial intelligence.

On the final day of the summit, Melania Trump and Figure 03 walked the red carpet toward the White House East Room. The humanoid robot then navigated around a table occupied by distinguished guests, including France's First Lady Brigitte Macron and Sierra Leone's First Lady Fatima Maada Bio, before positioning itself at the center of the room. After scanning the audience, Figure 03 delivered greetings in multiple languages, introducing itself: "Thank you, First Lady Melania Trump, for inviting me to the White House. It is an honor to be at Fostering the Future Together’s global coalition inaugural meeting. I’m Figure 03, a humanoid built for the United States of America. I am grateful to be part of this historic movement to empower children with technology and education."

Figure 03 is a product of Figure AI, a California-based startup that unveiled the humanoid robot in October 2025. Figure AI's mission is to develop general-purpose humanoid robots capable of performing dangerous, dull, and dirty jobs, with Figure 03 initially designed to assist with household tasks. Its high-profile appearance at the White House served as a powerful demonstration of the expanding role of AI-driven robotics in public diplomacy, international collaboration, and the future of educational technology. Mrs. Trump notably remarked, "It’s fair to state, you are my first American-made humanoid guest in the White House," underscoring the advancements in domestic robotics.

This event offers the global tech community a glimpse into the current state of cutting-edge AI hardware and human-robot interaction. It foreshadows a future where humanoid robots may extend beyond industrial applications to integrate into social services, education, and even high-level political engagements. The integration of such advanced technology into a diplomatic setting also sparks broader discussions on the ethical considerations, societal impacts, and future trajectories of AI and robotics.
