Google Photos on Android Launches "AI Enhance" Button Globally with Automated Lighting, Contrast, and Video Speed Controls

April 8, 2026 · News
google photosai enhancementandroidmobile ai

Google has officially launched a new "AI Enhance" button for its Photos application on Android devices globally. This significant update integrates advanced artificial intelligence capabilities to streamline and automate photo and video editing processes for users worldwide.

The primary features introduced with the "AI Enhance" button include automated lighting and contrast adjustments for photographs. Users can now simply tap the dedicated button to allow the application's AI algorithms to analyze their images and suggest optimal visual enhancements, thereby improving overall image quality and aesthetic appeal. Furthermore, the update brings new controls for video playback speed, offering users more granular control over their video content and enabling them to adjust the pacing as needed.

This global rollout underscores Google's ongoing strategy to embed powerful AI functionalities into its flagship products, aiming to simplify content creation and editing workflows. For developers and AI professionals, this move exemplifies the practical application of AI in enhancing user experience within consumer-facing applications, particularly in the domain of image and video processing on mobile platforms.
