Database Startup Supabase in Talks for New Funding Round, Valuation Could Hit $10 Billion

April 3, 2026 · News
postgresqlopen-sourcebackenddeveloper toolsvector database

Supabase, a prominent open-source database solution provider often recognized as a robust alternative to Firebase, is currently in active discussions with potential investors for a new funding round.

According to recent reports, if these negotiations are successful, the funding round is expected to double Supabase's valuation to an impressive $10 billion.

This potential substantial increase in valuation underscores strong market confidence in Supabase's technology, particularly within the developer tools and backend services sectors, and highlights its significant position in the rapidly expanding cloud services market.

The successful completion of this funding round would undoubtedly provide substantial capital for Supabase's future product innovation, community development, and global market expansion, further solidifying its status as a leader in open-source cloud infrastructure.
