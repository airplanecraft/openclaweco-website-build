OpenAI Launches GPT-5.4-Cyber for Enhanced Cybersecurity; Expands Trusted Access

April 15, 2026 · News
openaigpt-5.4-cybercybersecurityai-agentstrusted-access

OpenAI announced on April 14 the official launch of GPT-5.4-Cyber, an iterative model derived from its GPT-5.4 series. This new model is primarily designed to significantly enhance artificial intelligence capabilities within the cybersecurity domain.

Concurrently, OpenAI also revealed the expansion of its "Cybersecurity Trusted Access" program. The program is now open to carefully vetted and verified cybersecurity professionals and teams, aiming to foster a collaborative environment where advanced AI technologies can be leveraged to collectively address increasingly sophisticated cyber threats.
