AI Transforms Consulting: Silicon Valley Startups Raise Over $300M, Paving New Pathways

April 5, 2026 · News
ai consultingai startupsdata managementtechnology optimizationpromptql

Artificial intelligence (AI) is fundamentally disrupting the consulting industry, a sector that has seen little change for generations. Over the past year, Silicon Valley has witnessed the emergence of a new wave of AI-powered consulting tech startups.

These innovative companies are focused on leveraging AI to assist businesses in critical areas such as data management and technology optimization. Notably, four of these burgeoning firms have collectively secured over $300 million in funding, underscoring significant investor confidence in AI-driven consulting solutions.

Prominent figures in this evolving landscape include Bianca Anghelina, CEO of Aily Labs, and Tanmai Gopal, CEO of PromptQL, whose ventures exemplify the application of AI in transforming enterprise consulting practices.
