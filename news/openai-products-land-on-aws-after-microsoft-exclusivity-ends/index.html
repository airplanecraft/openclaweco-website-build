OpenAI Products Now Available on AWS as Microsoft's Exclusivity Ends, Signaling Broader Cloud Access

April 29, 2026 · News
openaiawsai modelscloud platformsdeveloper tools

OpenAI recently announced a revised partnership agreement with Microsoft, effectively terminating Microsoft's exclusive rights to its products. This development quickly prompted a strong response from Amazon.

Following the announcement, Amazon CEO Andy Jassy tweeted, labeling it an "intriguing announcement." This new agreement removes previous collaboration hurdles, enabling Amazon Web Services (AWS) to fully list OpenAI's range of products. The move is particularly significant given a prior partnership agreement between Amazon and OpenAI, potentially valued at up to $50 billion, where product collaboration permissions had been a prominent issue. The lifting of Microsoft's exclusivity now clears the path for deeper integration on AWS.
