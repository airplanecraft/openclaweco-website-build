Alibaba's Wan2.7 Video Generation Model Tops DesignArena Rankings, Significantly Outperforms Grok Imagine

April 10, 2026 · News
wan2.7designarenavideo generationvideo editingalibaba

Alibaba's recently launched Wan2.7 large video generation model has achieved a significant milestone, topping the DesignArena global crowdsourced evaluation platform with an Elo score of 1334. This performance is particularly notable in its Video to Video (video editing) capabilities, where it significantly outperforms its closest competitor, Grok Imagine, by 68 points (Grok Imagine scored 1266).

DesignArena serves as the world's first benchmark platform for crowdsourced evaluation of AI-generated designs, with its rankings determined by global user votes. Wan2.7's ascent to the top underscores its leading position in the video generation domain.

The Wan2.7-Video series of models was introduced just a week prior, encompassing four core models: text-to-video, image-to-video, reference-to-video, and video editing. This comprehensive suite aims to provide extensive creative control, expanding AI's capabilities from mere single-material generation to the entire creative workflow, effectively shifting from "performing" to "directing." Its video editing feature has garnered considerable attention, allowing users to make precise localized or global modifications to videos with just a single-sentence command.
